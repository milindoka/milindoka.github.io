How You Can Add Data

Have you spotted a rare bird? Do you know a traditional recipe using a local ingredient? We need your knowledge!

  1. **Observe & Document:** Take a clear photo of the species or record the traditional knowledge (name, use, location).
  2. **Note Details:** Write down the date, time, and exact location (if possible, GPS coordinates). Note the local name and any known uses.
  3. **Submit to BMC:** Send an email to the address above or submit a written report to the Village Panchayat office.
  4. **Verification:** The BMC will verify the information with our experts and formally add it to the digital and printed PBR.
