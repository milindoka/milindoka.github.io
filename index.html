Welcome to the People's Biodiversity Register

This is an initiative by the Village Panchayat and the Biodiversity Management Committee (BMC) of Burambad to document and celebrate the incredible flora, fauna, and traditional knowledge of our Konkan village. The PBR is a vital document for local conservation and sustainable use of our resources.
